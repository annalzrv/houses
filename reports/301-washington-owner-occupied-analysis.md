## Method and sources

The analysis follows the same method used for 61 Greene. Property facts come from NYC public records (Department of Finance, PLUTO, GeoSearch) and are listed with their source and confidence in the fact-sheet CSV. The property tax is the adopted FY2025-26 figure and was verified two independent ways. Rents are drawn from current furnished listings in Clinton Hill on Blueground and NY Habitat, cross-checked against local rent indices. Operating expenses other than property tax are estimates and are flagged as such; they should be replaced with the building's actual insurance, water, and utility bills.
